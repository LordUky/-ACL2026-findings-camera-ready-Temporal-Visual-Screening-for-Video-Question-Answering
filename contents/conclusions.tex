\section{Conclusion}
\vspace{-0.4em}
We introduce Endomorphic Multimodal Compression (EMC), a cognitively inspired task that reconstructs each VideoQA instance into an answer-preserving compact form. We realize EMC via ReSimplifyIt, a model-agnostic multi-agent baseline, and release EMCompress as its benchmark. Across models and datasets, EMC strengthens both training-time alignment and inference-time robustness, positioning information compression as a key direction for video-language understanding.
