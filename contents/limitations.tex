\section*{Limitations}

While information compression brings considerable performance gains, limitations remain.
Firstly, as the name suggests, endomorphic multimodal compression is only applied on the temporal axis and is unable to filter redundant spatial visual information. We recognize spatial visual compression as the more difficult counterpart of our task.
Secondly, while our plug-and-play framework adapts to any VideoQA agent, its end-to-end counterpart---Video-LLMs with inter-frame reasoning or query-adaptive frame sampling built into the encoder---is left unexplored. Both external adapters and end-to-end integration can advance information compression.
