\section*{Limitations}

Firstly, endomorphic multimodal compression is applied only on the temporal axis and cannot filter redundant spatial visual information; spatial visual compression remains a harder open problem.
Secondly, while our plug-and-play framework adapts to any VideoQA agent, its end-to-end counterpart---Video-LLMs with inter-frame reasoning or query-adaptive frame sampling built into the encoder---is left unexplored.
